Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that purchasing, showcasing, or commissioning art can raise a lot of questions. To make your experience with SCRL Fil-Art Studio smooth and enjoyable, we’ve compiled answers to some of the most frequently asked questions.

1. What is SCRL Fil-Art Studio?

SCRL Fil-Art Studio is a creative space that showcases and sells the works of talented local Filipino artists. The studio serves as a platform to promote Philippine art, culture, and heritage by providing exposure and opportunities for emerging and established artists.

2. Do you offer shipping and delivery for purchased artworks?

Yes, we offer both local and international shipping. For domestic orders, delivery takes 3-5 business days.For international orders, estimated delivery may vary depending on the destination. Shipping fees are calculated upon checkout.

3. How can local artists collaborate or showcase their works at SCRL Fil-Art Studio?

We welcome local artists who want to showcase their art! Interested artists can submit their portfolios and proposals by Emailing us at info@SCRL_ArtStudio.com

4. Does SCRL Fil-Art Studio offer art workshops or events?

Yes! We regularly host art workshops, exhibit launches, and cultural events that engage the community and celebrate Filipino creativity.

5. Do you support emerging artists and student creators?

Absolutely! SCRL Fil-Art Studio is dedicated to nurturing young talent and giving student artists a platform to showcase their skills. We also hold mentorship programs and open-call opportunities for emerging creators.
